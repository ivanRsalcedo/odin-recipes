Tropical Power

Home Berry Blast Tropical Power Double Chocolate Mint

Description

525 calories, 58 grams protein, 12 g fat, 46 g carbs, 8.5 g fiber. It's like a pina colada, but with 58 grams of protein and a cup of spinach.

Ingredients

  • 12 oz water, milk, or yogurt
  • 2 scoops vanilla flavored protein powder
  • 1/2 banana
  • 1 cup of pineapple
  • 1 cup of spinach
  • 1 tbsp of ground flax
  • 2 tbsp of unsweetened coconut flakes
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt or vegan alternative

Steps

  1. Pour 12 oz of water, milk, or yogurt into your blender.
  2. Add 2 scoops of vanilla protein powder.
  3. Drop in 1/2 banana for creaminess.
  4. Add 1 cup of pineapple.
  5. Toss in 1 cup of spinach.
  6. Sprinkle in 1 tablespoon of ground flax.
  7. Add 2 tablespoons of unsweetened coconut flakes.
  8. Finish with 1/2 cup of plain yogurt.
  9. Blend until smooth.
