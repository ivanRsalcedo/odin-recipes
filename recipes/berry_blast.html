Berry Blast

Description

500 calories, 57 g protein, 54 g carbs, 14 g fiber, 11 g fat. This shake is packed with protein, fiber, healthy fats, phyto-nutrients, and probiotics. It can be breakfast, lunch, or dinner.

Ingredients
  • 12 oz water
  • 1 cup spinach
  • 2 cups frozen mixed berries
  • 1/2 cup plain low-fat yogurt
  • 2 scoops plant protein powder
  • 1 tbsp walnuts
  • 1 tbsp ground flaxseed
Steps
  1. Add 12 oz of water to your blender.
  2. Toss in 1 cup of spinach for a green boost.
  3. Add 2 cups of frozen mixed berries for flavor and antioxidants.
  4. Scoop in Â½ cup of plain low-fat yogurt for creaminess and probiotics.
  5. Drop in 2 scoops of plant protein powder.
  6. Add 1 tablespoon of walnuts
  7. Sprinkle in 1 tablespoon of ground flaxseed for fiber and omega-3s.
  8. Blend until smooth.
