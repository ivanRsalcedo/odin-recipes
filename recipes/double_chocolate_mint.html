Double Chocolate Mint

Home Berry Blast Tropical Power Double Chocolate Mint
Description

292 calories, 25 g protein, 12 g fat, 32 g carbs. A decadent shake that tastes like dessert but fuels like a fitness boost, with rich chocolate, crunchy cacao, and a fresh minty twist.

Ingredients
  • 1 scoop chocolate protein powder
  • 3/4 cup Silk Almond Milk (dark chocolate)
  • 1 tbsp walnuts
  • 2 tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 tbsp cacao nibs
  • 2 mint leaves
  • 4 ice cubes
  • 1/4 cup water
Steps
  1. Add 1 scoop of chocolate protein powder.
  2. Pour in 3/4 cup of almond milk.
  3. Add 1 tablespoon of walnuts.
  4. Add 2 tablespoons of cocoa powder.
  5. Add 1 tablespoon of cacao nibs.
  6. Add 2 mint leaves.
  7. Add 4 ice cubes.
  8. Pour in 1/4 cup of water.
  9. Blend until smooth.
